About Us

G.Pulla Reddy Engineering College

G.Pulla Reddy Engineering College (Autonomous), the pride of Kurnool town, was founded by the renowned philanthropist Sri G. Pulla Reddy. Established in 1985, the college has become a premier institute for technical education, known for its discipline, value system, and uncompromising quality. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure and a rich history, GPREC continues to set benchmarks in education.

Vignana Mela 4.0

Vignana Mela 4.0 is a vibrant science fair hosted by G.Pulla Reddy Engineering College, bringing together students, researchers, and enthusiasts to celebrate innovation and technology. This event features engaging exhibitions, interactive workshops, and exciting competitions designed to inspire creativity and learning among young minds.
